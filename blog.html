  1. Start with goals
  2. Create systems to achieve those goals
  3. Believe in yourself
  4. Believe you are in control of your life
  5. Still embrace "random"
  6. Find happiness in the success of others
  7. Use your goals to make decisions automatic
    • "Indecision is born of a lack of purpose"
  8. Don't multi-task
    • "Do one thing incredibly well, then move on to what's next and to *that* incredibly well."
  9. Freely ask for help
    10. Want to be remarkably effective?
